# Find places of worship

This page shows you all prayer spaces near your address.

### Search

- Type **address** of place where you would like to find prayer spaces.
- or use your gps location by clicking on ![icon](/static/icons/maps/location.svg#icon) icon
- or click anywhere on the map

<!---  additionaly you can chose a search engine. defaults to ![icon](/static/icons/pow/masjid.svg#icon) --->

![Search bar](img/search.png)

### Map

Map shows 50 closest prayer spaces to your address. You can click on each icon for further details.
- ![icon](/static/icons/expand.svg#icon) show map in full screen
- ![icon](/static/icons/add.svg#icon) zoom in 
- ![icon](/static/icons/minus.svg#icon) zoom out 
![Search bar](img/map.png)

### List

List shows you details for each prayer spaces.

- Click on title to go to prayer space page to view all the details
- Upcoming events show first two events coming up. View prayer space for more events.
- ![icon](/static/icons/phone.svg#icon) call prayer space
- ![icon](/static/icons/maps/directions.svg#icon) diriving directions to prayer space
- ![icon](/static/icons/maps/globe.svg#icon) view prayer space website
