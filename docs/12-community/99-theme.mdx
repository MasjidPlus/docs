# Customizing look

Please note that our app matches your device settings, so if you have night mode enabled on your pc or mobile device, then our site will display accordingly.

However if you like a customized look, you can click on user avatar in top right corner, then click on settings.

![Theme mode](./img/user-settings-button.png)

Some settings will be disabled on mobile devices.

![Theme mode](./img/user-settings-drawer.png)
